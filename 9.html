Producer/Videographer

Intothewoods.tv, 2011-2014

Various Films and Videos



Founded 2010 in Portland, Oregon.

Intothewoods.tv was a nonprofit production agency that created documentaries, takeaway shows, and video shorts for hundreds of musicians and artists in and beyond the Pacific Northwest. Our content was released and featured on NPR, Vice, Pitchfork, and dozens more.
